Vintage-LuxBikes.com

Welcome to Vintage-LuxBikes.com. After doing a lot searching on the internet for information on vintage bikes, I noticed that there was some information out there but it was never as complete as I would like. My hope is to make this a great reference so if any one is looking for information on 1987- 1989 Schwinn Circuits this will be the place!

I love vintage lugged columbus steel bikes. I stumbled across the "Tenax" Schwinn bikes and was amazed at the quality of the frames and components. However, my web searches did not provide very good details about them. So, I decided to pull together all the good information - including my own measurements and observations. I hope you enjoy!

As always Vintage-LuxBikes.com is always looking for data, ads and reviews to add to its online archive. If you have something please contact me.
